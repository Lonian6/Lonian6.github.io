abstract: >-
    We establishes a fair-play benchmark that requires participants to train generative models strictly from scratch using a standardized, CC-licensed subset of the MTG-Jamendo dataset containing only instrumental music. The challenge is divided into two tracks: the Efficiency Track (limited to 500M parameters) and the Performance Track (no parameter limit). Submissions are evaluated through a multi-stage process (FAD, CLAP, CCS, MOS). By providing open-source baselines, preprocessing pipelines, reference captions, and public evaluation code for computing FAD and CLAP, this challenge aims to facilitate and promote TTM research in academic contexts.
